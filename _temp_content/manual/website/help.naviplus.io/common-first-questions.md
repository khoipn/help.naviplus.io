### IV. Performance & Scalability

#### 1. Is Navi+ lightweight?

Navi+ is extremely light:\
• First load: 2KB file (\~50ms) + libraries under 100KB (\~200ms)\
• Subsequent loads: Instant (0ms) due to caching\
Total impact on first visit: <250ms.

#### 2. Can Navi+ ever go down due to extremely high traffic?

Only if both Cloudflare and BunnyCDN are down simultaneously — probability \~0.00001%.

#### 3. Can Navi+ handle high traffic with low latency?

Yes. All menus are served directly from CDN edge locations — infinite scalability.

#### 4. Are menu updates published instantly?

Yes. Updates propagate globally within 20–60 seconds through CDN caching.

### V. Security

#### 1. Is my data safe with Navi+?

Navi+ does not store business details, product data, or sales information. It only stores menu visit and click data (organized by embed ID, device, timestamp) for Analytics.

#### 2. Does Navi+ comply with GDPR or privacy standards?

GDPR compliant. No personal data collected. All data encrypted. Never shared.

#### 3. Can hackers modify or inject code into the menu?

No, unless your login credentials (username/password, Google, Shopify) are compromised.

#### 4. What happens if my website is attacked — does Navi+ stay online?

Yes. Navi+ operates independently. Even if your site faces DDoS, SQL Injection, or phishing attempts, Navi+ menus continue functioning normally.
