# Navi+ AI overview

Navi+ integrates AI to help you design, build, and translate menus faster — without editing every item by hand.

Open the AI features from the bottom toolbar of the menu editor: click the **AI** button (or **⋮ More → AI Design**).

---

## Available AI features

| Feature | Use when | Plan |
|---|---|---|
| [Auto design using your AI app](/docs/ai/auto-design-your-ai/) | You already use ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini and want full control | Free |
| [Auto design by Navi+ AI](/docs/ai/auto-design-navi-ai/) | You want to design a menu in one step, with Navi+ handling the AI | Business / Elite |
| [Create menu structure](/docs/ai/create-menu-structure/) | You want to describe a menu and let AI build the initial structure | Free |
| [Auto-translate menu](/docs/ai/auto-translate/) | You need your menu shown in multiple languages | Business / Elite |

---

## Which feature should you use?

- **Prefer to fine-tune each AI prompt yourself?** → [Auto design using your AI app](/docs/ai/auto-design-your-ai/). Navi+ generates the prompt, you paste it into your favorite AI tool and bring the result back.
- **Want it fast, in one step?** → [Auto design by Navi+ AI](/docs/ai/auto-design-navi-ai/). Describe your request right inside Navi+ and the AI returns the result in place.
- **Starting from scratch?** → [Create menu structure](/docs/ai/create-menu-structure/). Describe the menu you need and AI builds a skeleton for you to refine.
- **Menu done, need multiple languages?** → [Auto-translate menu](/docs/ai/auto-translate/).

---

## Always back up before applying

Every AI feature **overwrites** your current menu when you apply the result. Back up your menu first:

> **⋮ More → Backup / Restore** — save as a downloadable file or to Navi+ cloud.

That way, if you don't like the AI result, you can restore your old menu right away.
