# Auto design by Navi+ AI

Let Navi+ handle all the AI work — just describe the menu you want right in the editor. No external tools, no copying and pasting JSON. *(Business / Elite)*

Open: the **AI** button → **Auto design by Navi+ AI** card.

---

## How is it different from "Auto design using your AI app"?

| | Your AI app | Navi+ AI |
|---|---|---|
| AI tool | You open ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini | Navi+ calls the AI for you |
| Copy/paste JSON | Yes, manually | Not needed |
| Steps | 4 steps | 1 step (chat) |
| Plan | Free | Business / Elite |

---

## Before you start — back up your menu

> **⋮ More → Backup / Restore** — back up your current menu before applying the AI result.

---

## How to use

1. Open the **Auto design by Navi+ AI** card.
2. Type a description in the chat box, e.g. "Create a 5-item tab bar for a fashion store, black and white."
3. (Optional) Attach a reference image with the **Upload** button so the AI follows the layout/colors.
4. Click **Send**. The AI returns a design and applies it to the editor for preview.
5. Keep chatting to refine ("change the Contact icon", "remove the last item")... or click **New chat** to start over.
6. Happy with it? Click **Save**.

> Your chat history is saved — click **History** to reopen earlier design sessions.

---

## Plan requirement

This feature is on the **Business / Elite** plan. On a lower plan you'll see an upgrade notice when you open this card.

## Case study — build a cosmetics tab bar in 3 chat turns

**Context:** A new cosmetics store with no menu yet wants a tab bar matching its pastel pink brand.

1. **Back up** first to be safe (⋮ More → Backup / Restore).
2. Open the **Auto design by Navi+ AI** card. Turn 1:

   ```
   Create a 5-item tab bar for a cosmetics store: Home, Products,
   Offers, Beauty Guide, Account. Pastel pink tones.
   ```

   The AI builds the tab bar and shows it in the preview right away.
3. Turn 2 — attach a sample UI image with the **Upload** button, then type:

   ```
   Match the corner radius and icons to the style in this image.
   ```

4. Turn 3 — a small tweak:

   ```
   Turn the Account item into a prominent Contact button with a phone icon.
   ```

5. Satisfied, click **Save**. Next time you need changes, click **History** to reopen this session.

**Result:** A tab bar that matches the brand, finished in 3 chat turns without leaving Navi+.
