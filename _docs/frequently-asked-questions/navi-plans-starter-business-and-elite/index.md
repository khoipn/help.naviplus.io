## Starter — Free

- Lifetime usage — $0/month, no credit card needed
- 1 Navi+ menu (any type: Tab Bar, Mega Menu, Slide Menu, Grid, FAB)
- Up to 50,000 Navi+ views
- Data analytics available for 30 days
- Infrastructure SLA 99.00%
- Email support
- Image upload not available — use icons instead
- Advanced configuration limited (Touch feedback, Navigation icons, Animation, Reusable Classes, Attributes)
- Ads may appear if usage significantly exceeds the limit — you'll receive an email notification before this happens

## Business — $6.25/month

Everything in Starter, plus:

- Up to 4 Navi+ menus
- Up to 500,000 Navi+ views/month
- Multi-language full support
- Standard photo upload — JPG, PNG, dynamic GIF
- Statistics and data analytics
- Open website UI components as popup: Search, Cart, Default menu
- Replace theme's Cart, Menu/Search icons (with cart count badge) and Chat bubble
- Advanced configuration: Touch feedback, Navigation icons, Animation style & speed, Reusable Classes, Attributes
- Good CDN — excellent performance for North America, Europe and Asia
- 24/7 support
- Ads may appear if usage significantly exceeds the limit — you'll receive an email notification before this happens

## Elite — $10/month

Everything in Business, plus:

- Unlimited Navi+ menus — multiple menus across pages, under different conditions, replace the entire default navigation
- Unlimited Navi+ views
- Advanced image tools: Convert to WebP · Image size normalization · External image URL
- SEO search optimization support
- Full data analytics — powerful insights to optimize your menu over time
- Comprehensive advanced configuration — everything in Business and deeper customizations
- Top-tier CDN worldwide — failover across Cloudflare + BunnyCDN, SLA 99.99%
- Never advertisements
- Hands-on support: our team can join your shop directly, migrate old menus, configure and edit UI on your behalf

## What happens when you exceed a limit?

**Navi+ will never disable or remove your menus.** We know your navigation is critical to your visitors — we won't break a live site.

Here's what actually happens:

1. **Email notification** — we send you a heads-up before taking any action.
2. **Ads may appear** — if you stay over the limit without upgrading, small "Powered by Navi+" ads may appear in your menus. They don't affect interaction but are visible enough for users to notice.
3. **No hidden features** — your menus keep working exactly as configured.

This applies to: Starter exceeding 50,000 views · Business exceeding 500,000 views/month · Downgrading while having more menus than your plan allows.
